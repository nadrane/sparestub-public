How It Works

Sell your stub and find a gig buddy

Have an extra ticket? Post it for free. List as Request to Buy to approve your gig buddy, or list as Quick Buy to accept the first buyer. Check out our Seller's Guide for more info!

Find a ticket and a new friend

Search for spare stubs in your city. Review the seller's bio and user reviews. Get to know each other. Match made in heaven?Request to Buy, or use Quick Buy if available. Check out the Buyer's Guide for more info!

Happily Ever After

The buyer and seller handle the ticket exchange, then have a great night with a new gig buddy! After the event, they can leave each other reviews and add to our wall of Success Stories!
