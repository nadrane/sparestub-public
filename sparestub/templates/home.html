How It Works

Sell your stub and find a gig buddy

  • Left with an extra ticket? Post it for free!
  • List your ticket as "Request to Buy" and approve your gig buddy, or list it as "Quick Buy" to accept the first buyer.

Find a ticket and a new friend

  • Search for something fun to do and review the seller's bio and user reviews.
  • Get to know each other. Match made in heaven? Time to buy!
  • Request to Buy the ticket, or use "Quick Buy" if available.

Happily Ever After

  • Once a buyer and seller are matched, they handle the ticket exchange and have a great night with a new gig buddy!
  • After the event, they can leave each other reviews and add to our wall of Success Stories!
