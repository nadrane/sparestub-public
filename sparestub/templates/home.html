How It Works

Sell your stub. Find a gig buddy

Have an extra ticket? Post it for free and watch the requests come in! Only pay the $5 charge if you approve a buyer. Check out our Seller's Guide for more info!

Find a ticket and a new friend

Search for spare stubs in your city. Check out the seller's bio and user reviews. Get to know each other. Match made in heaven? Request to Buy the ticket. Check out the Buyer's Guide for more info!

And Then... Happily Ever After

Buyers and sellers handle the ticket and payment exchange however works best for them, and then have a great night together! After the event, they can leave each other a review.
